Key Findings

Device Trends
- Newer devices (<5 years) are repurposed more frequently
- Older devices provide higher energy savings per device

Environmental Impact
- ~1.68 million kWh saved annually
- Equivalent to powering ~160 U.S. homes
- ~846 tons of CO₂ saved (≈ 184 cars off the road)

Regional Insights
- Asia shows highest CO₂ savings due to carbon-intensive energy
- Europe shows lowest CO₂ savings due to cleaner energy grid

Recommendations
- Prioritize older devices for higher energy savings
- Focus repurposing efforts in high carbon-intensity regions
- Extend lifecycle of newer devices to reduce manufacturing demand
